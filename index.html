The study of Belizean studies centers on the region that is both physically and culturally part of Belize. It explores the environmental, political, economic, social, cultural, and technical origins and evolution of Belize in a worldwide perspective using methods and concepts from anthropology, geography, history, philosophy, literature, and sociology. A secondary level course called Belizean Studies was introduced as a trial program in eleven high schools around the nation last year.
